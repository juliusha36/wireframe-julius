About Me

I currently work at PRA Health Sciences, a company that supports clinical researchers around the world advance new drugs to the market. There are a wide variety of clinical studies that the company supports, from cancers to blood disorders to rare diseases.

I find great satisfation in teaching and inspiring people, particularly the youth. For two summers, I had the privilege to work for Especially for Youth, a program of weekly summer camps that inspires high school-age boys and girls to live Christian values.

Another part of me wants to tackle great problems in the world. I worked as an undergraduate researcher at UVA investigating drug resistant malaria. I combined concepts of gene expression with computer science to find potential explanations behind malaria parasites that develop drug resistance.
